About bikes

Lifan Motorcycle Company, founded in 1992 in Chongqing, China, is a prominent manufacturer of motorcycles and related power equipment. With a strong commitment to innovation and quality, Lifan has established itself as a global player in the motorcycle industry. The company produces a wide range of motorcycles, from small scooters to powerful sport bikes, catering to diverse customer preferences. Lifan's products are known for their reliability, affordability, and adherence to international safety standards. Over the years, Lifan has expanded its presence in numerous countries, contributing significantly to the growth of the global motorcycle market and earning a reputation for producing durable and accessible two-wheelers.
